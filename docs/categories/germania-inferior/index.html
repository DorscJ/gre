Rural Villa at Neerhaaren-Rekem

Province Germania inferior Location Neerhaaren-Rekem Neerhaaren-Rekem (Pleiades) Location Description Sublocation Sublocation Description Garden Rural villa at Neerhaaren-Rekem Keywords rural villas villae rusticae arbors pergolas piscinae Garden Description The facade of this modest house of a villa rustica near the Meuse river measured 24 m. in length and had two protruding wings joined by a portico (Plan 1). The house was built in the late 1st century A.D. There is evidence for an arbor or pergola next to the house in the form of two rows of postholes arranged diagonally and converging in front of the entrance on the west (circles on plan).
